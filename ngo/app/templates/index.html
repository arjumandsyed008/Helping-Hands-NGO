Our Story

Helping Hands was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate individuals who wanted to make a tangible difference in the lives of the less fortunate. What started as a small local initiative has grown into an international organization with operations in 15 countries.

Our journey began with providing basic necessities to homeless individuals in our community. As we grew, we expanded our focus to address root causes of poverty through education, healthcare, and economic empowerment programs.

Today, we're proud to have impacted over 1 million lives through our various initiatives, and we're just getting started. Our work is made possible by the generosity of donors, the dedication of volunteers, and the resilience of the communities we serve.

Our Approach

Community-Led

We work with local communities to identify needs and develop solutions that are culturally appropriate and sustainable.

Evidence-Based

Our programs are designed based on research and data to ensure they effectively address the challenges we aim to solve.

Compassionate

We treat every individual with dignity and respect, recognizing the unique circumstances that led them to need assistance.
